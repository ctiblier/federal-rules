Closing arguments proceed in the following order: (a) the government argues; (b) the defense argues; and (c) the government rebuts.

<p class="amendment-history">(As added Apr. 22, 1974, eff. Dec. 1, 1975; amended Apr. 29, 2002, eff. Dec. 1, 2002.)</p>
