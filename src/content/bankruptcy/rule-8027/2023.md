**(a)** If the district court or BAP has a mediation procedure applicable to bankruptcy appeals, the clerk must notify the parties promptly after docketing the appeal of: the requirements of the mediation procedure; and

**(b)** any effect the mediation procedure has on the time to file briefs. (Added Apr. 25, 2014, eff. Dec. 1, 2014.)
