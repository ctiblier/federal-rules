Fed. R. Civ. P. 58 applies in an adversary proceeding. A reference in that rule to the civil docket must be read as referring to the docket maintained by the clerk under Rule 5003(a). (Added Mar. 26, 2009, eff. Dec. 1, 2009; amended Apr. 2, 2024, eff. Dec. 1, 2024.)
