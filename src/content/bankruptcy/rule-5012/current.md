An agreement to coordinate proceedings under § 1527(4) may be approved on motion with an attached copy of the agreement or protocol. Unless the court orders otherwise, the movant must give at least 30 days’ notice of any hearing on the motion by sending a copy to the United States trustee and serving it on: • the debtor; • all persons or bodies authorized to administer the debtor’s foreign proceedings; • all entities against whom provisional relief is sought under § 1519; • all parties to litigation pending in the United States in which the debtor was a party when the petition was filed; and • any other entity the court designates. (Added Apr. 28, 2010, eff. Dec. 1, 2010; amended Apr. 2, 2024, eff. Dec. 1, 2024.)
